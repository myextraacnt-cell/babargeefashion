What We Offer

Babar Gee Fashion provides premium boutique services including hand-embroidered suits, custom stitched outfits, and international order fulfillment. We cater to both Pakistani and international clients with quality and style, ensuring every piece reflects our commitment to traditional craftsmanship and contemporary elegance.

Custom Stitched Suits

Our custom stitched suits are crafted with precision and attention to detail. Each piece is tailored specifically to your measurements, ensuring a perfect fit that enhances your natural silhouette.

We use only premium quality fabrics including lawn, chiffon, silk, and cotton. Our experienced tailors bring decades of expertise to every stitch, creating garments that are both comfortable and elegant.

  • Perfect fit guaranteed with detailed measurements
  • Premium quality fabrics and materials
  • Traditional and contemporary designs available
  • Complete sets with dupatta and accessories

Hand Embriodery

Our hand embroidery work represents the finest tradition of Pakistani craftsmanship. Each design is meticulously created by skilled artisans who have mastered the art of needle work over generations.

From delicate floral patterns to intricate geometric designs, our embroidery adds elegance and sophistication to every garment. We use high-quality threads, beads, and embellishments to ensure lasting beauty.

  • Traditional hand embroidery techniques
  • Custom patterns and designs available
  • Premium quality threads and embellishments
  • Skilled artisans with decades of experience

Maxis & Lehenga Designs

Our collection of maxis and lehengas combines traditional Pakistani aesthetics with modern fashion sensibilities. Perfect for weddings, parties, and special occasions.

Each piece is designed to make you feel confident and beautiful. From flowing maxis for elegant evening wear to elaborate bridal lehengas with intricate work, we create memorable pieces.

  • Bridal and party wear lehengas
  • Casual and formal maxis
  • Custom color combinations available
  • Matching accessories and jewelry consultation

Bulk Orders & International Shipping

We specialize in bulk orders for boutiques, retailers, and fashion businesses worldwide. Our flexible minimum order quantities and competitive pricing make us the ideal partner for your business.

With reliable international shipping and careful packaging, we ensure your orders reach you in perfect condition. We've successfully served customers across UK, USA, Canada, Australia, and many other countries.

  • Flexible minimum order quantities
  • Competitive wholesale pricing
  • Reliable international shipping
  • Custom packaging and branding options
