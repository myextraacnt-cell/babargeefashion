Our Story

Founded in 1982 in the historic city of Sukkur, Babar Gee Fashion has been a cornerstone of Pakistani fashion excellence for over four decades. What began as a small boutique has grown into a trusted name in high-quality stitched suits, embroidered lehengas, and elegant maxis.

With 42+ years of hands-on experience in ladies fashion, our expertise spans intricate embroidery work, precision pattern making, and custom design creation. Every piece that leaves our workshop carries the legacy of traditional Pakistani craftsmanship combined with contemporary elegance.

Our commitment to quality extends beyond borders. We take pride in serving customers worldwide, delivering handcrafted elegance from Pakistan to doorsteps across the globe. From intimate custom orders to large bulk collections, each project receives our unwavering attention to detail.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality handcrafted Pakistani clothing worldwide, preserving traditional embroidery techniques while embracing contemporary designs. We strive to make authentic Pakistani fashion accessible to customers across the globe through exceptional craftsmanship and personalized service.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a trusted international brand for boutique Pakistani fashion, known for our commitment to quality, cultural authenticity, and customer satisfaction. We envision expanding our reach while maintaining the personal touch that defines our brand.

Our Journey

1982

Babar Gee Fashion Established

Founded in Sukkur with a vision to create exceptional Pakistani fashion. Started as a small boutique specializing in traditional stitched suits and embroidered garments.

2017

Expanded Boutique & Embroidery Services

Expanded operations to include advanced embroidery services and custom design capabilities. Began accepting bulk orders and established international shipping services.

2025

Serving Pakistani & International Clients

Today, we proudly serve customers across Pakistan and internationally, maintaining our commitment to quality while embracing digital platforms to reach fashion enthusiasts worldwide.
